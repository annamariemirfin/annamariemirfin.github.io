Traces, 2019

Traces draws together information on geology, hydrology, tectonics, drawings, poetry, personal anecdotes, history and philosophy relating to the Waiarariki / Eyre River, Canterbury. It takes the form of a three piece work - a research book which can be read in the gallery, a layered glass piece and a video work. It responds to the site as a place of personal connection and an understudied ecosystem.
